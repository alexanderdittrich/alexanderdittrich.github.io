Alexander Dittrich

PhD Student in Robot Learning at the Laboratory of Intelligent Systems at EPFL

Research

I'm exploring whether biologically inspired neural network architectures and learning approaches can improve the generalization capabilities and sample-efficiency of embodied systems in reward-based behaviour learning beyond what's achievable with current state-of-the-art reinforcement learning. We apply these architectures and learning approaches to large-scale autonomous and soft tensegrity robots in disaster scenarios like collapsed buildings and bridges.
